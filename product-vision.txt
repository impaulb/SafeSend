FOR individuals, and small business owners

WHO need a way to ensure sensitive information is not mistakenly sent via email and do not
have the budget for an in-house security team

THE SafeSend software is a Gmail plugin

THAT scans emails for sensitive information like credit card numbers, social security numbers, and other keywords and uses machine learning to redact this information and notify the sender

UNLIKE in-house security teams or more general Gmail plugins

OUR PRODUCT provides real-time, efficient scanning and customizable settings to ensure
sensitive information is not accidentally sent via email, giving users and managers peace of
mind that employees are not mistakenly endangering customer info
